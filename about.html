About us

ReputationPlus is a contemporary reputation and communication management company dedicated to helping businesses and individuals manage their engagements with stakeholders. We are equipped to provide strategic insight and advisory services that will facilitate attainment of defined goals. As a spry outfit, peopled by professionals drawn from the different areas of marketing communications, our services can be integrated into your marketing mix or deployed independently to engage your publics. Our operations are underpinned by the ethos of conventional public relations practice - reach and influence, while we are fully aware of the need to draw attention to our clients in the crowded marketplace. We are skilled at shaping narratives through appropriate messaging that resonates with target audience. At ReputationPlus, we leverage our cultural awareness, linguistic resourcefulness and extra-ordinary listening skills as components of broad communication strategies to put our clients in the spotlight and enhance the quality of their relationship with stakeholders.
