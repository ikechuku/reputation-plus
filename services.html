Services

Our social value is largely a function of the opinion others hold about us. Often times, this public estimation is subjective and not reflective of who we truly are, but unfortunately it remains the major lens through which the society interprets individuals, organisations, and brands. Paradoxically, it takes several good deeds to build a positive reputation, and just one misdeed to destroy a history of good deeds. Isn’t it logical to care about one’s reputation?
